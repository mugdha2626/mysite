Web3 app that converts food discoveries into tradeable on-chain ERC1155 tokens, rewarding users for identifying trending dishes early. Features GPS-verified discovery, bonding curve pricing, and gasless transactions via Coinbase Paymaster. Won 2nd Place in Base (EVM) Track at Midwest Blockchain Conference 2025 ($2,500 Prize).
